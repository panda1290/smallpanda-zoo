**Question : Both of them means 'if', what's the difference between them?**

a. たら
- た form - past tense
- 酒を飲んだら、運転するな (If you have drunk, don't drive.)
- Drunk - first action

b. なら
- 運転するなら、酒を飲むな (If you want to drive, don't drink beer.)
- Drink beer - first action
